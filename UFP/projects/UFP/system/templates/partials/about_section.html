About the Platform

The University Feedback Platform was developed by students to provide a centralized, accessible, and anonymous space where students can share their experiences about university services (Wi-Fi, Library access, Scholarships, and College Admissions) and also give feedback through teacher evaluation.

Using Natural Language Processing (NLP), the platform automatically analyzes the sentiment of student feedback. This covers both university services (Wi-Fi, Library, Scholarships, etc.) and teacher evaluation. The collected feedback is also processed as part of our thesis to build the sentiment analysis model and generate insights that can be used for future improvements.
