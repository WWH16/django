About the Platform

The University Feedback Platform was developed by students in collaboration with OSAS to provide a centralized, accessible, and anonymous space where students can voice their experiences regarding university services (Wi-Fi, Library access, Scholarships, etc.) and faculty performance.

Using Natural Language Processing (NLP), the platform performs automatic sentiment analysis to help the university understand how students feel about the services provided. The insights gathered support evidence-based improvements and better student engagement.
