Student Guide

How to Give Feedback for OSAS Services

  1. Visit the website.
  2. Click on "Login" (or "Sign up" if you don’t have an account).
  3. Select "Student Login".
  4. After logging in, choose the type of feedback from the side navigation bar:
    1. Give Feedback (OSAS Services) → Select a service (e.g., Wi-Fi, Library), then enter your honest feedback.
    2. Teacher Evaluation → Select a teacher and department, then enter your honest feedback.
  5. (Optional) Check “Submit as anonymous” if you don’t want your name attached.
  6. Click “Submit” — the system will analyze the sentiment automatically.

Tips:

  • Be honest and constructive.
  • Speak casually, no formality needed.
  • Do not include names or personal identifiers.
  • Take advantage of the anonymity.
  • Write at least 5 words in your feedback. Very short answers (like “Good” or “Bad”) are not enough for the system to understand the full context. A minimum of five words ensures the sentiment analysis is accurate and your feedback is meaningful for improvement.
Examples of Feedback

✅ Good: “The Wi-Fi is slow and keeps disconnecting.”

✅ Good: “The library is clean but sometimes noisy.”

❌ Bad: “Good only.”

❌ Bad: “Okay lang.”

❌ Bad: “Goods.”

❌ Bad: “Bad.”
