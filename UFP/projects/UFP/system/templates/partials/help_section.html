How to Give Feedback

1Visit the Website

Go to the platform URL to start the feedback process.

2Login or Sign Up

Click "Login" if you have an account or click "Guest" for anonymous feedback.

3Select Feedback Type

Choose from the side navigation bar:
Give Feedback (OSAS Services) → select a service (e.g., Wi-Fi, Library).
Teacher Evaluation → select a teacher and department.

4Submit Feedback

Write honest feedback, optionally check "Submit as anonymous," and click "Submit". The system will automatically analyze the sentiment.

Help Center

What is this website for? ▼
This platform enables students to provide feedback on both university services and faculty performance. The system analyzes sentiment automatically for insights.
Do I need an account to give feedback? ▼
Yes, creating an account ensures integrity and organization of feedback, but your identity is protected.
Who can see my feedback? ▼
Only authorized faculty or staff (admins) can view feedback and analysis.
Can I edit my feedback? ▼
No, feedback cannot be edited. Submit a new one if needed.
