Student Guide

How to Use the Feedback Form:

  1. Visit the website.
  2. Click on "Feedback".
  3. Select a service (e.g., Wi-Fi, Library).
  4. Enter your honest feedback.
  5. Submit — the system will analyze the sentiment automatically.

Tips:

  • Be honest and constructive.
  • Speak casually, no formality needed.
  • Do not include names or personal identifiers.
  • Take advantage of the anonymity.

Admin Dashboard (Faculty Only)

  • View feedback by sentiment: Positive, Negative, Neutral.
  • Access statistical reports per service.
  • Filter and visualize trends via charts and tables.
